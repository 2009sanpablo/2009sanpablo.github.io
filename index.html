Welcome to 2009 San Pablo Ave

Introducing the San Pablo Central property located at 2009 San Pablo Blvd., Berkeley, CA. This versatile retail building in the heart of Central Berkeley offers a unique opportunity for owner-users with its open floor plan, high ceilings, mezzanine, and a charming rear patio. Boasting a total of 6,396 SF, which includes 5,469 SF ground floor and a 927 SF mezzanine. The property features natural light, skylights, and a flexible layout that can accommodate various businesses. Situated just steps away from the bustling intersection of San Pablo Avenue and University Avenue, the location enjoys high visibility and convenient access to major transportation routes including CA-80 and BART. Surrounded by a mix of local and national retailers, the area presents an excellent business environment. Previously used as a CrossFit gym, this property is perfect for a dance studio, brewery with outdoor seating, or a design showroom, catering to diverse entrepreneurial ventures. Don't miss out on this prime commercial offering in a sought-after Berkeley neighborhood!
